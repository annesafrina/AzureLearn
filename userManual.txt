Azure PowerShell
To enumerate resource from the whole tenant:
Get-AzResource
To enumerate the name of the domain:
Get-AzDomain
To enumerate resource from the whole tenant:
Get-AzResource
To enumerate resource application in the tenant:
Get-AzADApplication
To enumerate blob storage in the tenant:
Get-AzStorageAccount
To enumerate key vault in the tenant:
Get-AzKeyVault
To enumerate the secret of a key vault:
Get-AzKeyVaultSecret -VaultName [name_of_key_vault]
To enumerate the role assignments:
Get-AzRoleAssignment -SignInName [email]
Az CLI
To check the current session of az CLI:
az ad signed-in-user show
To check owned objects of a user:
az ad signed-in-user list-owned-objects
To request for access token of the logged in user:
az account get-access-token
